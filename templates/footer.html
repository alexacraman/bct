About

TO PROVIDE A COMMUNITY TRANSPORT SERVICE FOR THE INHABITANTS OF BILLINGSHURST AND ITS NEIGHBOURHOOD WHO ARE IN NEED OF SUCH A SERVICE BECAUSE of SICKNESS OR DISABILITY (MENTAL OR PHYSICAL), OR LACK OF FINANCIAL RESOURCES, OR BECAUSE OF A LACK OF AVAILABILITY OF ADEQUATE AND SAFE PUBLIC PASSENGER SERVICES.
