Billingshurst Community Transport provides a service to elderly, frail and infirm residents who are unable to use public transport.

Volunteer drivers will use either their own cars, or drive a fully wheelchair accessible vehicle provided by the scheme.

Where appropriate a companion may accompany a passenger at no additional charge.The car or accessible vehicle will pick up passengers from their home, or any other location within the area served.

Rear access to wheelchair vehicle is via a ramp and hoist. Full restraining straps are fitted.
